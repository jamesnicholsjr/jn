SemiHealthy Life

Overview

SemiHealthy helps people who want to start taking small, specific steps towards a healthier lifestyle.

They were ready for a website that accurately reflected their brand. Using the Design Thinking method, I set out to design a solution that would meet the needs of the target user, the objectives of the company and technical capabilities.

Role: Lead UX Designer (research, interaction design, visual design)

Case Study

Research
With research goals in mind, I gathered contextual information about the heal market and identified a baseline for what users expect from a streaming service.
Findings
Interviews & Empathy Mapping Takeways:

a) Users want some value from services.

b) Users look for the most current, up to date information on their issues.

c) Users expect a service tailored to their individual needs.



Persona Development

The provisional personas created early in research were updated to reflect the depth of the insights gathered through primary research.



How Might We…..

Research insights became actionable problem statements that would help frame design considerations. How Might We questions were central to solution-oriented brainstorming.
Design
The design phases kicked off by planning the information architecture. Creating a sitemap ensured new features were placed in a flow that was intuitive. Over the course of a week, low-fidelity sketches…were translated into a web layout.
